About Huong

Huong comes from Vietnam, where she gained her first experience in the beauty industry in 2007. Since moving to Switzerland, she has attended various courses at well-known beauty schools. In 2009 she opened her first own studio under the name "Wonder Nails". Since 2014 she has been a trainer for nail arts courses at Yves-Swiss.

Since 2015, she has been intensively trained in microblading and permanent makeup at the Phibrows Academy. She is PhiBrow's Royal Artist and winner of the 2016 PMU International Championship in Seoul, South Korea. In the same year she opened her new White Lotus Beauty® Studio in Zurich. In July 2017, she was awarded the PhiBrows Master title. In spring 2018, she founded the Huong Chu Beauty Academy and her own brand HC Brows Switzerland. Since 2019 she is also Master Trainer for Microblading and Permanent Make-Up from Swiss Color International. Huong regularly gives microblading and permanent make-up training courses at home and abroad. Since autumn 2021, Huong has also been an officially recognised dermapigmentologist by the FOPH (Federal Office of Public Health).
